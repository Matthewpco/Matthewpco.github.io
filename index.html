A little about me

I've made my home base in Boulder, Colorado where I enjoy hiking the Rocky Mountains with my wife. My journey into tech started as a child with a love for video games, puzzle solving, and the dazzling array of sights and sounds. As I coded my first pong game in C#, I was hooked on the unlimited possibilites of development.

That curiosity opened a door that would lead me to study Computer Science in-depth at Harvard, and later Graphic Design at the University of Boulder. Now I have come full circle from learning the same design and development skills that first inspired me as a child, to building projects with an eye for detail, thoughtful design, and clean code.
